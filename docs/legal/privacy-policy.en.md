# CheerChoice Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-10

## 1. Who we are
CheerChoice is a meal and exercise support app operated by the CheerChoice team.

## 2. Data we collect
- Account and technical data: anonymous user ID, app version, device and error logs.
- App usage data: AI photo usage counts, exercise and meal records, settings.
- Image data: food photos that you submit for calorie estimation.

## 3. How we use data
- Provide core app features such as calorie estimation, logs, and statistics.
- Enforce free and premium usage limits.
- Improve reliability through crash and error monitoring.

## 4. Third-party services
- OpenAI: calorie estimation processing.
- Supabase: authentication and data storage.
- Sentry: crash and error monitoring.

## 5. Data retention
Data is stored as long as needed to provide the service or until you request deletion where supported.

## 6. Data sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers required to operate CheerChoice.

## 7. Your choices
- You may stop using the app at any time.
- You can request data deletion by contacting support.

## 8. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures, but no method is 100 percent secure.

## 9. Children
CheerChoice is not intended for children under 13.

## 10. Changes
We may update this policy and will update the "Last updated" date.
